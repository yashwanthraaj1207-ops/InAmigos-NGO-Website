About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a non-governmental organization licensed by the Central Government and registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013. Founded on 23 September 2020 in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, the organization works towards environmental conservation, education support, food distribution, community development, animal welfare and social upliftment.
